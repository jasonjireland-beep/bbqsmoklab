Frequently Asked Questions

My Bluetooth probe won't connect.

Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your iPhone and your ThermoPro TP920 probe is powered on and within range. Try closing and reopening the app, and confirm the probe isn't already paired with another device.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Is BBQ Smoke Lab Pro a subscription?

No — Pro is a one-time purchase. Once unlocked, it's yours forever with no recurring charges.

Which thermometers are supported?

BBQ Smoke Lab currently supports the ThermoPro TP920 wireless meat thermometer via Bluetooth.
